About Us

Welcome to Tree Plantation Foundation

We are a dedicated tree plantation foundation that has successfully crossed the milestone of planting over 10 thousand trees, and aim to plant over 1 lakh tress. What started as a humble initiative by a group of nature enthusiasts has now blossomed into a movement making a positive impact on the environment and local communities.

Our Journey

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to restore nature and combat deforestation by planting trees. Starting with a handful of volunteers organizing local community-based tree plantation drives, we faced numerous challenges. However, through perseverance, partnerships with local organizations, and the growing involvement of eco-conscious individuals, we have steadily grown over the years.

Today, we proudly stand at over 1 lakh trees planted, contributing to greener urban spaces, improved air quality, and enhanced biodiversity. We continue to push forward, believing that every tree planted brings us closer to a healthier and more sustainable planet.

Our Work

  • Tree plantation drives in urban and rural areas
  • Partnering with schools and local communities for awareness programs
  • Conservation efforts for local biodiversity and ecosystems
  • Workshops on sustainable living and eco-friendly practices

Our Team

Our success is fueled by a passionate team of volunteers, environmentalists, and community leaders. From the initial stages, where only a few members worked tirelessly, to our current team, which includes over 100 active members, we have grown into a force that inspires others to join the cause. We believe that teamwork and collective effort are key to our success.

Looking Forward

Our mission is far from over. As we look to the future, we aim to increase our plantation targets, expand our awareness programs, and involve more people in our journey toward a greener planet. Together, we can make a lasting difference.
